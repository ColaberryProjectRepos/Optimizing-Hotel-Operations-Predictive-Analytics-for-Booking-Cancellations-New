Methodology 4

Data Preparation

Data was cleaned by identifying and removing duplicates, handling missing values, removing outliers, and standardizing the data to ensure accuracy and support model development.



Model Selection

BernoulliNB classification model was chosen for its simplicity and effectiveness in handling binary features, which aligns with the 'Is canceled' dependent variable. The model achieved 100% accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score, reflecting perfect classification performance.


Model Development

The model was trained on historical hotel booking data, focusing on predicting cancellations (is_Canceled). This enables precise forecasting, crucial for optimizing hotel operations and resource management.
