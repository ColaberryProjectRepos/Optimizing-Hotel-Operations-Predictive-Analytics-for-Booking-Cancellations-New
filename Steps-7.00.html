Positive Insight:

Bookings made by repeat guests that include special requests show a 50% decrease in cancellations. This suggests higher satisfaction and commitment among this customer segment when their specific needs are met.




Negative Insight:

First-time guests without special requests have a 55% higher rate of cancellations. Lack of engagement or perceived value may drive this trend.





Case 2 ROI:
  • Implementing a targeted approach to increase special requests handling for first-time and repeat guests could reduce cancellations by 15%, potentially increasing revenue by 10% and providing an ROI of 30%.
