Lessons Learned and Future Work 9

Challenges and Solutions

  1. Limitation of data wrangler in Microsoft fabric to deal with noisy and incomplete historical booking data. I Implement robust data cleaning and preprocessing steps using MS SQL, Python and Power Query editor in the later stages to handle missing values, remove duplicates, and standardize data formats.
  2. Ensuring the classification model effectively generalizes to new, unseen data. I employed cross-validation techniques and hyperparameter tuning to optimize the model's performance and prevent overfitting.
  3. Communicating complex data insights and model predictions to non-technical stakeholders. I used Power BI to develop intuitive data visualization to create interactive dashboards that present key insights in an easily understandable format.


Lessons Learned

The project underscores the critical need for thorough data preprocessing and rigorous model validation to manage noisy data and avoid overfitting. Moreover, it emphasizes the importance of presenting complex insights through clear, intuitive visualizations to engage stakeholders and facilitate informed decision-making.


Future Directions

Although the project has successfully focused on lead time, market segments, customer types, and special requests using a classification model, there are additional areas to explore. Future directions include enhancing predictive model performance, conducting geospatial analysis of cancellations, analyzing customer behavior, and detecting seasonal patterns in cancellations.
